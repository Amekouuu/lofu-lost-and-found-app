Our History

LoFu was born from a simple observation of our local community in Angeles City. We noticed that while our neighborhoods are full of helpful people, there wasn't a dedicated, organized space to reconnect lost belongings with their rightful owners.

What started as a vision to simplify the "Lost and Found" process has grown into a mission to foster honesty and kindness. We realized that losing an essential item — whether it's a cherished heirloom or a daily necessity — is a stressful experience. We created LoFu to turn that stress into relief by providing a transparent, easy-to-use platform where neighbors can look out for one another.
